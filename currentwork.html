Sofia's Current Work

As a Senior at the University of Miami, she is approaching her fourth year of being involved in the student-run broadcast network on campus, UMTV.

It is her second semester as executive producer and editor of the only spanish-speaking show on the network, UniMiami. She has acted as reporter, anchor, and crew member for UniMiami since her freshman year and finds it very fulfilling that she has been able to work her way up to this leadership position.

As she nears graduation in December she hopes to continue to work in the industry, as a producer, with the dream of someday working for a station in New York. Linked is a reel with her most current work.
