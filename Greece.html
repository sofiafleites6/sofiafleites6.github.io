Greece is a country in Southeast Europe that is made up of a chain of islands throughout the Aegean, Ionian and Mediterranean seas. Most of the population lives in and around the capital, Athens, and the rest of the population is sprawled around the islands and other urban areas. The country is conventionally known as "Hellenic Republic" and was emancipated from the Ottoman Empire in the mid 1800s. Since then, Greece was occupied by both Italy and Germany in World War II, and in the 60s the country was seized by a group of military officers, leading to a military dictatorship that lasted well into the 1970s. In 1981, Greece joined the EU, and joined the Economic and Monetary Union in 2001, becoming their 12th member. The country entered three bailout agreements, having suffered a severe economic crisis due to "chronic overspending and structural rigidities." The first two bailout agreements were with the European Commission, the European Central Bank and the IMF; and the third was with the European Stability Mechanism. Greece exited their third bailout in August 2018.

Geography

Greece is located in Southern Europe, between Albania and Turkey. As stated in the introduction, the country's islands border the Aegean Sea, Ionian Sea, and the Mediterranean Sea.

The Greek Government

Government type

parliamentary republic

Capital

Athens,the oldest European capital city, is said to have been named after the goddess Athena. However, the word "Athens" is most likely derived from a lost word in the pre-Hellenic language.

Constitution

The latest revision of the Constitution was done in June of 1975. Amendments must be proposed by at least 50 members of Parliament and agreed by three-fifths majority vote in two separate ballots at least 30 days apart; passage requires absolute majority vote by the next elected Parliament; entry into force finalized through a "special parliamentary resolution"; articles on human rights and freedoms and the form of government cannot be amended. Amendments have been made in 1986, 2001, 2008, and 2019.
