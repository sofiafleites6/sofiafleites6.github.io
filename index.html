On-Camera Experience

Since she was a freshman at the U, Sofia has been a part of the student run organization on campus, UMTV. She worked her way up the ranks, first as a reporter for Pulse, the entertainment show on UMTV, and eventually became the spanish news anchor her junior year. She worked diligently and dedicated herself to the club throughout her four years as an undergraduate student.

Production Experience

Her senior year, Sofia was promoted to executive producer of the spanish show on UMTV. As an executive producer she was able to create a new show from scratch which included, writing, casting, and directing every episode throughout the season. Though she loved being on camera, producing was a new challenge for her and she quickly grew to love it more than on-camera work.
